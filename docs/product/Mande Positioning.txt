**How it works**  
Mande works by seamlessly integrating into users' daily routines to comprehensively capture their professional journey. Users write down their daily experiences, like achievements, tasks, lessons, and challenges, creating a kind of digital micro-journal. Mande uses AI to study this information, giving regular tips and spotting patterns to suggest personalized advice for moving ahead in their careers. It's like having a helpful guide that not only looks at what's happened but also helps plan what comes next. With Mande, users get more than just a tool; it's a friendly companion that understands their work life, offering insights and guidance for a successful career journey.
